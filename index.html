Here is a little about me and my family.


Erin (that's me!)

My name is Erin, and I live in St Louis, MO and I'm learning to code. This page is a chance for me to learn somethings and tell you a little about my family. I was born in St Louis and I've lived here almost my whole life -- I spent 4 years at Syracuse University getting my degree. I started college set on becoming a photojournalist but graduated realizing that wasn't the life for me.

I still loved photography so I applied for commercial photographer positions and was hired to be the in-house photographer for a ready-to-assemble furniture company. Through a variety of business and financial twists and turns I ended up art directing and managing more photographs than I actually took there. I also began assiting the graphic designers when they needed help and slowly earned a position as a graphic designer.

Now I'm working for an Office Supply Company as a Senior Graphic Designer and taking this awesome LC101 class so I can learn how to code. Way back in high school I did some html classes and really enjoyed it. I worked on a big website overhaul at work and I just had to learn more about what the developers were talking about, so here I am, learning!
